Privacy Policy & Cookie Disclosure

At Expressionbird, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

  1. Information we collect

    We collect information that you provide directly to us and data that is collected automatically:

    • Personal Data: Name, email address, and phone number (collected via contact forms or newsletter sign-ups).

    • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, and pages visited (collected automatically to help us improve our site).

  2. How We Use Your Information

    We use your data to:

    • Respond to your inquiries about our marketing services.

    • Send you newsletters or promotional materials (only if you opt-in).

    • Analyze website traffic to improve the user experience of Expressionbird.

  3. Cookie Policy

    Our website uses "Cookies" to enhance your experience. Cookies are small files stored on your device.

    • Why we use them: To remember your preferences, understand how you use oursite, and assist with our marketing efforts (such as Google Analytics).

    • Your Choice: You can choose to accept or decline cookies through your browser settings or our on-site cookie banner. Note that disabling cookies may affect some website features.

  4. Sharing Your Data

    We do not sell your personal information. We only share data with trusted third-party service providers (like email hosts or analytics tools) who help us run our business, and only as much as is necessary.

  5. Your Rights

    Depending on your location, you have the right to:

    • Access the data we have about you.

    • Request that we delete your personal information.

    • Opt-out of marketing communications at any time.
